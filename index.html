The idea

Reading to your kids is one of the best parts of being a parent.

And it disappears the moment it's over. Catherine's Corner keeps it. A parent, a grandparent, an aunt, a big sister reads a favorite book aloud once, and your child can hear it whenever they want. At bedtime. During the day while you're at work. Years from now, when they just miss you.

How it works

Three simple steps.

1

Read and record

Open the app, hold the book, and just read. No buttons to fuss with, no camera to juggle. You read your child their story the way you always do, and we keep it.

2
📖 📖 🖍️

Line up the pages

Afterward, take a photo of each page (or a drawing your child made), tap along as you listen back — and type the words once, if you'd like them on screen. Pages and words then follow the voice, for every reading of that book.

3
G D M

They listen, anytime

Your child opens their shelf, picks whose voice they want to hear, and follows along with the words. Grandma's version, Dad's version, all in one place.

Why it's different

Made to be treasured.

🎙️

Their real voice

The people your child loves, reading the books your child knows by heart. The funny voices, the pauses, all of it.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family only

Only the people you invite can record. No strangers, ever. It's a private corner that belongs to your child.

📖

Read along

Type a page's words once and they appear on screen as the story is read — lighting up gently with the voice — so your child can follow along and grow into reading.

🌙

Kept for good

These readings are yours to keep, and yours to download. They stay safe, even as life changes.

How it compares

Everything in one place.
